Project Overview

My logo represents a fictitious educational technology organization called “evolved.” The concept is to bridge the gap between classroom learning and career readiness, helping learners develop future-ready skills. The “ED” in evolved emphasizes education, and the “l” is shaped like a tie for professionalism.

I used two overlapping triangles to form a fast-forward symbol, representing progress and technology. Multiple horizontal lines add texture and motion. The color palette includes teal, gray, and white for trust, growth, and modernity.

Overall, the logo communicates growth, innovation, and the organization’s mission to empower learners through educational technology.
