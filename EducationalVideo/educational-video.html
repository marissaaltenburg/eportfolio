Project Overview

I focused on networking and promoting the Gator Network platform, making content educational and inspiring. I wrote a script opening with a “Did you know…?” question and statistic, and closed with a reflective question to encourage ongoing engagement.

I storyboarded the video using still images, screen recordings, and stock videos. Most frames were 3–5 seconds with transitions and gentle animations to maintain flow and visual interest. Background music, narration, and on-screen captions were layered last.

The result is an engaging, visually cohesive, and educational video promoting networking through Gator Network, with accessible visuals and clear messaging.
