Project Overview

I produced a 3-minute narrated software screencast in Camtasia to show how to right-align dates on a résumé in Microsoft Word. I scripted the walkthrough, recorded visual highlights, and explained each step clearly for hands-on learning.

Animated callouts and zoom effects were added to reinforce key points. The project leverages clean visuals, structured narration, and interactive design to teach a formatting trick that improves résumé readability and looks professionally polished.
